# Privacy Policy for Solstice Cipher

**Effective Date**: June 13, 2026

At **Solstice Cipher**, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our players. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle user data in our game.

## 1. Information We Collect
**Solstice Cipher** is a local, client-side puzzle game.
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect, request, or store any personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or locations.
- **Local Save Data**: The game stores puzzle progress, unlocked levels, and configuration settings locally on your device. We do not transmit this save data to any remote servers.

## 2. Permissions
Our application requests **zero device permissions** on Android. It does not require access to your camera, microphone, contacts, storage, or internet.

## 3. Children's Privacy
Because we collect no personal information of any kind, our game is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions or feedback about this privacy policy, please contact the developer directly.
