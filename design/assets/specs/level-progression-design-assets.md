### Technical Artist Flags:
1. **Memory Bloat:** "Lossless/VRAM Uncompressed" is extremely dangerous for WebGL exports. OOM crashes are highly likely. We should reconsider this for the Web export.
2. **Transformations:** "Nearest Filtering" with Mipmaps OFF will result in severe aliasing if sprites are scaled non-integer or rotated dynamically. We must snap to integers/fixed angles.
3. **Portals:** SubViewport nodes for true rendering of portals are intensely expensive in HTML5. We must impose a strict cap on active Portals if they use SubViewports.
