# Game Vision: Solstice Cipher

## 1. Core Fantasy
You are a master of light, bending optics and predicting trajectories to decode the ultimate cipher. 

## 2. Unique Hook
Mind-bending optical physics (reflection, splitting, filtering) layered with cryptographic logic to create a pure brain-burning puzzle experience.

## 3. Target Aesthetics (MDA Framework)
1. **Challenge:** Pushing spatial logic and deductive reasoning to the limit.
2. **Sensation:** The highly satisfying, instant visual feedback of light beams aligning and symbols glowing.
3. **Discovery:** Understanding the spatial relationships and learning new optical mechanics as they are introduced.

## 4. Emotional Arc
Curiosity ➡️ Contemplation/Experimentation ➡️ The "Aha!" Moment ➡️ Anticipation for the next mechanical twist.

## 5. Anti-Pillars (What this game is NOT)
* **Not time-pressured:** No countdowns or twitch reflexes required.
* **Not arbitrary:** All solutions follow strict, observable rules of light physics.
* **Not punishing:** No fail states or game-overs; just the pursuit of alignment.
* **Not story-heavy:** No heavy narrative exposition. The focus is purely on the mechanical puzzle.
